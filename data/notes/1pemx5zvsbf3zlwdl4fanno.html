Humans have authority over creation

(5) Yet you have made him a little lower than the heavenly beings
and crowned him with glory and honor.
(6) You have given him dominion over the works of your hands;
you have put all things under his feet,

Psalm 8:5-6 (Psalm 8)

God gave us authority over everything in this world. We are basically second in command, directly instituted by God.

That authority also comes with a responsibility to take good care of the earth, a job which once could say we haven't done all too well anymore during the last 100 years or so considering issues like environmental pollution, extinction of animals, global warming etc.
