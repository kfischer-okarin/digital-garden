God is the one who changes hearts

Create in me a clean heart, O God,
and renew a right spirit within me.

Psalm 51:10 (Psalm 51 (Private))

David wrote this as part of the psalm he wrote after one of the biggest mistakes he ever made in his life. He knew that he himself cannot produce a right heart attitude that is pleasing to God.
