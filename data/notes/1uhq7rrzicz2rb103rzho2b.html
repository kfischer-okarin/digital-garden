Parametrize tests

Single argument

@pytest.mark.parametrize("color", ["red", "blue", "green"], id=str)

Multiple arguments

@pytest.mark.parametrize("height,width", [(20, 30), (40, 10)], id=str)

Custom human readable ids

@pytest.mark.parametrize(
    "height,width",
    [
        (10, 5),
        (400, 200)
    ],
    ids=[
        'small size',
        'big size'
    ]
)
