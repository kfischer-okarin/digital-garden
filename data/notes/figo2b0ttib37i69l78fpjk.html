Genesis 46

ESV

My Summary

  • Jacob has a vision of God in which he is told that he doesn't need to be afraid to go to Egypt and that his descendants would eventually come back to this land
  • All members of Jacob's family that end up in Egypt are listed
  • Jacob and his son Joseph finally reunite

Central verses for me

Then he said, “I am God, the God of your father. Do not be afraid to go down to Egypt, for there I will make you into a great nation. I myself will go down with you to Egypt, and I will also bring you up again, and Joseph's hand shall close your eyes.”
