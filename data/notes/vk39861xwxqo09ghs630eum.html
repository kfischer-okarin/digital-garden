ROC Curve

  • Receiver Operating characteristic curve
  • It assumes you have a classification model with some kind of "threshold" you can adjust
    • You can nudge it towards classifying data more easily as positive - which in turn also increases false positives or the other way round
  • Plots False Positive Rate (x) vs True Positive Rate (y)
